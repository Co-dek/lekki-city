Lekki City, Lagos

Lekki is one of the most breathtaking attractions located in Lagos. It's home to a number of waterfalls, summer outdoor recreation, and poplar Nigerian artistes. Modern glass houses and sky scrappers are a significant part of the area's reputation.

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling through the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light
